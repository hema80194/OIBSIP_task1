About Us


Best Western Hotel Hebron is one of Copenhagen's oldest hotels and was inaugurated in 1898 and has always been privately owned.Throughout the hotels long History,we have maintained an oak tree as part of our logo,because even today we want to send signals about durability,unwaveringness and strengthens part of our core values.

We look forward to having you as a guest and we hope you will enjoy your stay with us.All our employees will do their utmost to make you feel good.



Conferences


If you need to hold conferences or courses,then the Best Western Hotel is Hebron,which is ideal place within its central location in the city.The good atmosphere at the hotel doesnot cost extra.so book your meetings in the Best Western Hotel Hebron safely,and our profeesional team will be ready to welcome you.

The hotel has 7 meeting rooms with modern standara AV equipment,air-conditioning and free wifi, and with room for 50 meeting participants in a theatrical setting.
